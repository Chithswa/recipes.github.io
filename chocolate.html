Delicious Chocolate cake Recipe
Chocolate Cake

A chocolate cake is a cake made with chocolate or cocoa. The ingredients are put into a bowl and mixed together; then the mixed ingredients are put into a cake tin and put in an oven until it is fully cooked. Then the cake is taken out of the oven and placed on a cooling stand. If the cake maker wants to, they can ice the cake, when it has cooled down, by spreading thick icing on it. Chocolate cakes are sold in shops as well as made at home. They are a sweet food and should not be eaten all the time, which would be unhealthy.

  • prep time

    30mints

  • cook time

    45mints

  • Servings

    6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting and enjoy!
