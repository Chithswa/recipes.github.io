Delicious Cheesecake Recipe
Cheese Cake

A cheesecake is a dessert cake made of cheese (usually cream cheese but sometimes ricotta), eggs, and sugar. It often has a graham cracker crumb crust, sometimes held together by melted butter. The filling has a very heavy, smooth texture. It sometimes has sour cream to make it easier to shape and may have fruit flavoring such as strawberry, blueberry, or lemon. It is often topped with fresh fruit or chocolate.

  • prep time

    30mints

  • cook time

    15mints

  • Servings

    5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Mix in the sour cream and flour until well combined.
  6. Pour the batter over the crust in the springform pan.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the center is almost set. Let it cool in the oven with the door slightly open.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving. Enjoy!
